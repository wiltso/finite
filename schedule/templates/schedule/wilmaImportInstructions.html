1. Open up your own Wilma account in web browser. (does not work with the app!)

2. Go to the “schedule” (“Työjärjestys”, “Schema”) tab in Wilma.

3. Find the small blue box that has an arrow going out of it. (It is next to the print icon)

4. Click on the box-icon.

5. And copy the link it creates for you and displayed in the middle of the yellow box

6. Then come back to Finite

7. Go to the import your schedule tab.

8. Then just paste the link there.

9. Press submit, and you are done!
